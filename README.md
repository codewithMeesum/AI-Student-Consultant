# AI-Student-Consultant
Get personalized career guidance based on your academic performance, study habits, and skills. Let AI help you make informed decisions about your future.
# Description 
AI Consultant Web App : 
I am currently developing an AI-powered student career guidance platform using Emergent AI. The system will help students receive personalized career suggestions based on:
• Matric & FSc Marks
 • Study Hours & Study Habits
 • Skills and Interests
 • Career Preferences
This is an early preview and I’m excited to share that the platform will be fully functional within a week!
Looking forward to your feedback and suggestions as I continue improving it.
